About Me

About Me

Hello, I’m Xue Li, and you may call me Liv as well.

I am a passionate data scientist with a PhD degree in Astrophysics and Cosmology. I have worked as a post-doctor researcher at Tsinghua University and earned my PhD at the University of Copenhagen.

Feel free to contact me at lixue.liv@outlook.com.

Press Coverage

  • 2014: “GRBs: A New Standard Candle?” - An article in Sky & Telescope Magazine introducing the work of “Light Curve Properties of Supernovae Associated With Gamma-ray Bursts” by Xue Li and Jens Hjorth (2014).

Selected Research Projects

  • Supervised Student for Supernovae Classification with AI

    • Provided guidance on AI and machine learning theory and applications.
    • Collaborated on data collection, ML model building, and data analysis using R, TensorFlow, and Python.

  • Led Netizen Behavior Analysis Project

    • Conducted web scraping and social media analysis.
    • Applied web scraping techniques to collect real-time social media data.
    • Utilized machine learning techniques like TensorFlow for netizen behavior modeling.
    • Employed natural language analysis and statistical methods (R and Python).

  • Scientific Project on AT2018cow

    • Designed the scientific approach and applied advanced mathematical and statistical tools.
    • Analyzed time series data using Python, Matlab, and SQL.
    • Presented findings at the American Astronomical Meeting in 2022.

  • Contributed to Scientific Project on SN 2018oh

    • Utilized Matlab and Python for data analysis on supernova data.
    • Identified the origin of supernova explosion. The project was highlighted by NASA.

  • Led Scientific Project on Gamma-Ray Burst Supernovae (GRB-SNe) and Cosmology

    • Utilized Matlab, IDL, Python, and R for calculations and data analysis on GRB-SNe time series data.
    • Pioneered the identification of intrinsic trends and patterns in GRB-SNe light curves.
    • Applied them to measure cosmological parameters. The project was highlighted by Sky & Telescope Magazine.

  • Led and Contributed to multiple Scientific Projects

    • Focused on data analysis and data science in the field of scientific research.
