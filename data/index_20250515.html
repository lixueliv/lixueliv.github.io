  2. Supernova Classification with ML

    • Supervised a student in a study of supernovae classification using ML. Provided guidance on machine learning theory and applications.

  3. Time Series Analysis of AT2018cow: Black Hole Discovery

    • Initiated and led a scientific project on AT2018cow, where I was the first to discover that AT2018cow was a black hole with quasi-periodic oscillations.
    • Designed the scientific approach, applied mathematical and statistical tools, including the Lomb-Scarglo periodogram, Fast Fourier analysis, Monte Carlo simulations, cross-correlation functions, and other advanced statistical methods to analyze time series data.
    • Presented these groundbreaking findings at the American Astronomical Meeting in 2022.

  4. Multidimensional Analysis of SN 2018oh: Origin of Supernova Explosions

    • Contributed to a scientific project on SN 2018oh.
    • Utilized Matlab and Python for data analysis on supernova data.
    • Identified the origin of supernova explosions. The project was highlighted by phys.org.

  5. Data-Driven Discovery: Utilizing Numerical Calculations for Gamma-ray Burst Supernovae as Standard Candles

    • Led a scientific project on gamma-ray burst supernovae (GRB-SNe) and cosmology.
    • Utilized Matlab, IDL, Python, and R for calculations and data analysis on GRB-SNe time series data.
    • Pioneered the identification of intrinsic trends and patterns in GRB-SNe light curves and applied them to measure cosmological parameters.
    • The project was highlighted by Sky & Telescope Magazine.
